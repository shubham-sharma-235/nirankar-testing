Crafting Legacy: The Manyawar Shri Kanshiram Ji Green Eco Garden, Lucknow

  • Client: Uttar Pradesh Government
  • Expertise: Monumental Public Space Stonework
  • Stone: Raw Pink Sandstone from Rajasthan
  • Elements: Domes, Colonnades, Elephant Statues, Landscaping
  • Location: Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh

Crafting Legacy: The Manyawar Shri Kanshiram Ji Green Eco Garden, Lucknow — Nirankar Sandstone Industries proudly contributed to this monumental public space built in honor of social reformer Kanshi Ram. Designed as a cultural and civic landmark, the garden features expansive stone architecture and finely crafted elements that enrich its serene environment.

From large-scale structures to detailed sculptures, our craftsmanship played a key role in defining the character of this iconic project. The use of premium sandstone, expert carving, and thoughtful integration helped create a space that is both visually grand and spiritually grounding for lakhs of visitors.

What We’ve Done

Our work combined precision engineering, traditional carving, and large-scale architectural execution to bring this landmark to life.

  • Supply of raw pink sandstone sourced from Rajasthan for structural longevity and aesthetic appeal.
  • Architectural stone integration for the grand dome, colonnades, pathways, and open plazas.
  • Handcrafted elephant statues and carved stone elements enhancing cultural narrative and visual appeal.
  • Landscape stonework for pathways, edging, and open spaces to support the park’s expansive layout.
  • Coordination with onsite teams to ensure accuracy, alignment, and seamless installation.

Preliminary Designs or Layouts

Before beginning stone supply and carving, our team reviewed layout plans for circulation, dome geometry, walking paths, and statue placement. Sample blocks and finish options were tested to ensure uniformity across large areas and maintain harmony with existing architectural vocabulary.

Creative Process

Creating large public monuments requires both scale and sensitivity. Each statue and carved element was shaped through iterative sketching, proportion checks, and surface finishing techniques. The result reflects a balance between monumental grandeur and artistic refinement.

Building a Successful Client-Architecture Relationship

Regular site visits, collaborative detailing with engineers, and timely supply cycles ensured smooth execution. The process remained transparent and adaptable, creating trust and efficiency across teams working on multiple zones simultaneously.

Project Results

The Manyawar Shri Kanshiram Ji Green Eco Garden stands today as a tribute to legacy, culture, and craftsmanship. The stonework enhances its identity as a peaceful public space while offering durability for decades of civic engagement, gatherings, and tourism.
