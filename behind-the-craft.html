Meet the Artisans Behind Our Masterpieces

At Nirankar Sandstone Industries, our artisans are the heart of every creation. Generations of craftsmen transform raw stone into timeless works of art — blending precision, patience, and passion passed down through decades of tradition. Their hands shape heritage into form, giving each piece its soul.

01

Stone Selection & Planning

We begin by selecting premium sandstone blocks and planning sizes, finishes, and quantities as per project drawings.

02

Cutting & Primary Shaping

Raw blocks are cut on gang saws and machines into slabs, columns, and profiles, ready for carving and detailing.

03

Hand-Carving & Finishing

Skilled artisans hand-carve jalis, jharokhas, arches, and moldings, followed by fine finishing and surface treatment.

04

Quality Check & Dispatch

Every piece is checked for size, finish, and detailing, then securely packed and dispatched to project sites worldwide.

Operational strengths that power every project.

In-house stone processing

Integrated cutting, shaping and finishing under one roof ensures better control, coordination and faster turnaround for complex stone projects.

Skilled artisan workforce

A dedicated team of artisans and technicians blends heritage hand-carving with modern methods to deliver intricate jalis, jharokhas and temple elements at scale.

Advanced machinery & tech

CNC routers, gangsaws and polishing lines enable precision sizing, uniform finishes and repeat accuracy across large façade and structural stone orders.

Stringent quality control

Multi-level checks for size, finish and carving depth, along with dry-fitting when required, ensure each stone reaches site ready to install.

Secure packing & logistics

Custom crating, edge protection and organised palletisation help minimise breakage and keep shipments project-wise, easing on-site handling.

Safe & sustainable workplace

Dust-control systems, safety practices and long-term artisan relationships support a stable, responsible factory ecosystem for every project.
